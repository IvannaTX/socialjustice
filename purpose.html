Our Vision:

A healthcare system committed to the awareness and elimination of health disparities, the facilitation of health and equal access to quality health and health care resources regardless of race, ethnicity or socio-economic status.

Our Mission:

To increase the capacity of underserved racial and ethnic communities across the U.S. to achieve health equity.

Our Purpose:

The DADH is committed to achieving racial and ethnic health equity. Racial/Ethnic Groups We Serve: Hispanic/Latinos African Americans/Black Asian Americans Pacific Islanders Native Americans Caribbean Islanders
