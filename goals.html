Our Main Goals


Our ultimate goal to reduce the disparities in patient care that prevent individuals from receiving appropriate care for their needs. We aim to:

Goal No. 1

Our first goal is to raise awareness to to the disparities in healthcare.

Goal No. 2

Have doctors/physicians and all forms of healthcare employees to pledge to practice medicine without implicit bias.

Goal No. 3

Change the curriculum to be more inclusive of specific needs of minorities, so that have better quality of healthcare for all.

Goal No. 4

Make sure every person feels safe and secure while being in a medical/healthcare environment.
