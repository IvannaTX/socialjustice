Mission Statement

We believe that if we raise awareness, educate, and pledge to eliminate disparities in healthcare, we decrease and ultimately eliminate their occurrence entirely.

Goals

Our ultimate goal is to reduce the disparities in patient care that prevent individuals from receiving appropriate care for their needs.

Our Main Goals


Our ultimate goal to reduce the disparities in patient care that prevent individuals from receiving appropriate care for their needs. We aim to:

Goal No. 1

Our first goal is to raise awareness to to the disparities in healthcare.

Goal No. 2

Have doctors/physicians and all forms of healthcare employees pledge to practice medicine without implicit bias.

Goal No. 3

Change the curriculum to be more inclusive to specific needs of minorities, so they have better quality of healthcare.

Goal No. 4

Make sure every person feels safe and secure while being in a medical/healthcare environment.
